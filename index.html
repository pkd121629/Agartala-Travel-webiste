Welcome To Agartala

Visit

Agartala sets a perfect example of the blend of natural splendor and rich history. The place has its name even in the scriptures of Mahabharta, which makes it an important place in the history of Hindu as well. At the time of British rule, the city was called as the princely state. Everything has contributed to the making of Agartala of today and tourists who plan their visit to this city often come back to learning a lot about history, tribes, cultures, and many more. Being a part of the seven-sister state, Agartala is different from many other tourist places. There are many places to visit in Agartala which will definitely give you a different perspective.
